It’s nice to meet you!

In January 2021 I graduated from Wroclaw University of Science and Technology with a Bachelor of Science in Architecture. In 3.5 years I have learned a lot about architecture design, but also about designing in general, because as Don Norman wrote in “Design of everyday things”:

“(...) the fundamental principles of designing for people are the same across all domains. People are the same, and so the design principles are the same.(...)”

Since then, I have been doing an internship as a Web Designer in the e-commerce company: Media Ambassador. Since December 2019 I’ve been a member of CodersCrew: non-profit organization operating in Wrocław, where I’m a part of UX/UI Design team. I am also in a group of after work enthusiasts called Otasoft, where we are working on booking engine for Online Travel Agency. I’m interested in design as a process of solving problems, which could make a change in a human (not only) life as in the whole world.
I’m really into architecture, fantasy literature, cats, art and many, many more. I'm a constant learner, there is so much amazing staff to learn!
